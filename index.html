About

This project focuses on developing a Self-Driving Lab (SDL) for the autonomous closed-loop synthesis of gold nanoparticles. By integrating robotics, machine learning, and automation, we aim to accelerate materials discovery and enable scalable nanoparticle synthesis with precision and reproducibility.
